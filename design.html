Literature Review: Key Research Takeaways

  • Transformer Models (BERT): Significantly improve the accuracy of detecting mental states from text.
  • Labeled Datasets: Therapist-labeled, local datasets yield much higher real-world reliability than generic social media data.
  • Multimodal Input: Combining text and voice data (prosody, tone) can improve detection accuracy.
  • Key Takeaway: Reliable prediction is feasible, but requires modern models fine-tuned on high-quality, therapist-labeled local data.
